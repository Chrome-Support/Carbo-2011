I've diffused into the aquatic plant.  I'm feeling that it's a bit cramped in here as I enter the chloroplast of a cell.  What's happening to me is  becoming clear, I'm going through the process of photosynthesis.  In this process, energy from the sun is being used to change me, along with water drawn up from the roots, into a simple sugar molecule (C6H12O6).  Both sugars, and the starches they are sometimes converted into are know as carbohydrates. Oxygen gas is also formed and released into the water.  This is really pretty interesting.  I wonder what will happen next.
