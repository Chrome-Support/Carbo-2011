I've entered the leaf.  It's cool and refreshing in here, though a bit cramped.  It looks like I'm entering the chloroplast of a cell.  What's happening to me.  It's becoming clear, I'm going through the process of photosynthesis.  In this process, energy from the sun is being used to change me, along with water drawn up from the roots, into a simple sugar molecule (C6H12O6).  Now I'm being joined with other simple sugars to form large starch molecules.  Both sugars and starches are know as carbohydrates. Oxygen gas is also formed and released into the atmosphere.  This is really pretty interesting.  I wonder what will happen next.
