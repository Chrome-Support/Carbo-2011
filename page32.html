Even though I had been eaten by a fish before, I had never heard of "fishing" before.  What a thrill.  My fish gave quite a fight, but in the end he was no match for Mr. S. and Mr. C.  That afternoon we were cooked on a grill and eaten.  Down into the digestive tract I went.  After being digested, I diffused into the blood.

What should I do?

I could GO THROUGH THE PROCESS OF CELLULAR RESPIRATION
-or-
I could REMAIN IN THE PERSON UNTIL DEATH DO US PART
